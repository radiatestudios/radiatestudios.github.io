About Radiate

Radiate Studios combines traditional creative workflows with cutting edge 3D design and printing to create innovative mixed media works. Based in Canada's Okanagan, Radiate aims to explore the convergence of the natural and virtual in the artistic space.

Sunbeam Collection

This series explores the ever-changing relationship between light, shadow and perspective. Combining traditional wall-mounted canvases with depth added via 3D printing, the result is finished with a variety of paints and mediums to achieve dynamic works that reacts to the viewer's position and perspective as well as the surrounding environment.
