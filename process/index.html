About The Process

Marrying the accuracy of 3D printing and design technology with the vast range of colours and textures afforded via traditional paints and mediums.

Designs are created to the works dimensions and optimized for production. The frame is then 3D printed, assembled and fastened to the base or canvas.

Works are finished using a variety of paints and mediums to define the colour palette and final textures.
